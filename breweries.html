Glasgow

WEST's state of the art brewery resides within the winding house of the magnificent Templeton Building overlooking Glasgow Green and produces award-winning, German-style lagers and wheat beers.

Alloa

Steeped in Scottish tradition the Williams Brothers have been guardians of the ancient Gaelic recipe for "Leann Fraoch" (Heather Ale). Their latest release "Totemic Tallboys" are packed with flavour.

Fallen Brewing Co.

Stirling

Fallen Brewery brew in the old Kippen railway station in Stirlingshire. Their outlook and love of full-flavoured beer ensure they combine a progressive approach with a healthy respect for tradition and heritage.

71 Brewing

Dundee

Dundee's only brewery have been crafting crisp lagers and seasonal beers inspired by progressive new world flavours and traditional classics since 2016. Driven by a creative spark their beers are matched by quality and consistency.

Stewart Brewing

Edinburgh

Edinburgh's original craft brewery founded in 2004, has seen demand soar since their inception meaning they now have a custom built premise which is catered to the public. A visit here with their onsite Taproom is not to be missed.

Highlands

Fierce

Aberdeen

Aberdeens first micro-brewery, FIERCE make flavour packed beers of all varieties. With their eye-catching marketing, these beers will turn your fridge into a modern art installation.

Loch Ness Brewery

Aviemore

Originally situated on the shores of the mysterious stretch of water, home of "Nessie", their new home in Aviemore means they still get their Highland water from the Monadliath Mountains, allowing maximum flavour to be extracted from the malts & hops.

Black Isle Brewing Co.

Munlochy

Black Isle founder David Gladwin set out to make world class beer using barley and hops grown on organic farms without the use of destructive chemicals. Today they brew up to 10,000 litres a day, and you can find their beer all over the world.

Cromarty Brewing Co.

Davidston | Cromarty

Founding brewer Craig began the art of beer making in student accomodation and flat basements. Since then he has re-established the Cromarty brewery delivering completely non-pasteurised or filtered beer packed with flavour.

Six Degrees North

Aberdeen

Six Degrees North brew classic belgian inspired beers, as well as modern, hop-forward styles. The brewery gets its name from the founders love of Belgian beer culture and tradition - and the brewery is situated Six Degrees North of Brussels.

Isle of Skye

Skye brewery was born was some friends met in a local pub and conversation turned to the lack of good beer on Skye. Three years later they were ready to brew their first ale - Red Cuillin, which is the same recipe we see now in Skye Red.

The Orkney Brewery

Orkney Isles

Only the finest ingredients and purest Orcadian water can create these outstanding, full-of-flavour ales. So next time you sip one of their brews, you can be sure it was made with the best malt, hops, yeast, Orcadian water — and a long history of making quality ales.

Arran Brewery

Isle of Arran

Situated off the West coast of Scotland the Isle of Arran is one of the most beautiful and dramatic islands in Europe. Using the island’s unique water and only the finest natural ingredients the brewery produces a wide range of beers and ales to suit every palette.

On Islay, men and women have been changing barley from the fields into world-class beers and whiskies since the age of the Vikings. We are a small, traditional 4-barrel brewery. We rely on experience, taste and aroma to guide our brewing process.
