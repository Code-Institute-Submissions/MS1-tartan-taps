FAQ's

Click on questions to see the answers.


How often do I receive a crate?

A new crate will be sent every month.

How will I know when I receive my beer?

We will email you in advance of every delivery to let you know you're beer is ready to sent and what it contains.

Can I change the date my beer is delivered?

Yes. You can contact us anytime to change the date of delivery, prior to the cut-off date stated in the advance email.

Can I change the crate size?

Yes. You can contact us anytime to change the amount of beers you receive, prior to the cut-off date stated in the advance email.

How much does delivery cost?

Nothing. Delivery is included in the cost of your subscription

Are there any hidden fees or cancellation costs?

No. There are no hidden fees, and no additional costs to pause or cancel your subscription

How do I cancel or pause my subscription?

Simply fill out the form letting us know how long you wish your subscription to be paused for, or if you wish to cancel. Alternatively give us a call. We will send email confirmation of any changes made to your subscription.
